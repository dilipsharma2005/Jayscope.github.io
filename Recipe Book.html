Halwa

  • Time: 10 min's
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Atta 100 g
Sugar 20 g
Water 1 glass
Ghee 1 tableSpoon

Step 1: Put Ghee in a pan.

Step 2: Then put Atta in it.

Step 3: Mix it well for until it turns brown.

Step 4: Now add sugar and cook for 5 min's on low flame.

Step 5: Serve hot.

Chicken Biryani

  • Time: 9 min's
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 5 leg peices
Chopped Onion_ 2 Onions
Green Chillies 2 Chillies
Rice 1 Bowl
Oil 2 tableSpoon
Cardamon As per taste
Cinemon As per taste

Step 1: Spread oil in the pan.

Step 2: Put Cinemon in the pan ,now put cardamon and chillies then fry it well for two minutes.

Step 3: Put onion in it and mix it well until turns brown.

Step 4: Now put Chicken in it and fry the Chicken until turns dark brown.

Step 5: Now put rice in it and mix it well for 5 minutes.

Step 1: Serve hot.
